      DISINFECTING ELECTROSTATIC SPRAYING

      At Renue Systems, we employ state-of-the-art electrostatic spray technology to deliver superior disinfection results for your facility. Our cutting-edge electrostatic spraying method ensures that disinfectant is efficiently and effectively applied to all surfaces, including those that are often overlooked or difficult to reach.
       

      Best-in-Class Technology:
       

      Our electrostatic spraying system utilizes top-of-the-line equipment, ensuring optimal coverage and penetration of disinfectant on surfaces. The electrostatically charged particles adhere to surfaces in a uniform and comprehensive manner, providing complete disinfection across your facility.
       

      Efficient and Effective:
       

      With our advanced electrostatic spraying technology, we can disinfect surfaces quickly and efficiently. The electrostatically charged particles wrap around surfaces, reaching even hidden or hard-to-access areas that traditional cleaning methods may miss. This results in thorough disinfection, reducing the risk of illness and promoting a healthier environment for occupants.

      ​

      Comprehensive Disinfection:
       

      From high-touch surfaces to intricate nooks and crannies, our electrostatic spraying technique ensures that every area of your facility receives thorough disinfection. Whether it's door handles, light switches, or other frequently touched surfaces, our method guarantees comprehensive coverage, providing peace of mind to you and your occupants.
       

      Enhanced Safety Measures:
       

      Renue Systems prioritizes safety in all our disinfection procedures. Our electrostatic spraying technology allows for precise and controlled application of disinfectant, minimizing chemical exposure and ensuring the safety of occupants and cleaning staff alike.
       

      Experience the Renue Systems Difference:
       

      With Renue Systems, you can trust that your facility is in capable hands. Our advanced electrostatic spraying technology, combined with our expertise and commitment to excellence, ensures that your space receives the highest standard of disinfection. Contact us today to learn more about our electrostatic spraying services and schedule a consultation tailored to your facility's needs.
