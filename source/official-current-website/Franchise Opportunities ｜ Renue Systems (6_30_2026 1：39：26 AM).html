      Consider a Unique Opportunity

      Are you looking for a highly specialized franchise business with a strong track record, tremendous name recognition within its high-end business customer base, a leadership position in an attractive niche and a reputation for providing world-class quality services while receiving tremendous support from the franchisor who views you as their partner?

      ​

      We are looking for solid, winning professionals we can count on and who are interested in joining our team to capitalize on our unparalleled customer relationships using our proven systems. With Renue Systems, you will collaborate with us to serve high-profile customers in the growing hospitality industry. Benefiting from our ongoing support, you will help them maintain their properties to exacting cleanliness standards, so they will achieve the highest guest satisfaction scores and increase their profitability. And you will benefit from:

      ​

      • Your own exclusive and large territory, where you are a preferred vendor to a wide variety of customers

      • Affiliation with a growing franchise system that gives you extensive sales, operational and business-building support

      • Working in a market leadership position in a sector with strong barriers to competition

      • Serving as both a cost-saver and revenue generator for your customers

      Start Your Business Immediately

      We give you a complete, turnkey business package, which includes the finest proprietary equipment, chemicals and supplies, as well as full training, marketing assistance and business building support. You will be up and running quickly.

      No specific experience is necessary since our systems are straightforward and uncomplicated to learn. And you gain the advantages of:

      ​

      • Low start-up cost and minimal overhead consisting primarily of a van, modest storage facility and small team of technicians

      ​

      • Strong customer base of leading hospitality properties – many who have chosen Renue as their preferred vendor

      ​

      • Unique and specialized business that customers increasingly opt to outsource

      ​

      • Extensive training and on-going support at our corporate home, your location and your customer prospects’ premises

      ​

      • Limited competition as no other company has our scope of services, reputation for quality, relationships, geographic footprint, experience and name recognition

      ​

      • Deep industry expertise with proprietary lines of chemicals, equipment and methodologies

      ​

      • Large and expanding market that demands an increasingly higher quality service performed by an outside company

      ​

      • Innovative franchisor that works closely with its franchisees to frequently introduce new services and customers to enable them to continue to grow
