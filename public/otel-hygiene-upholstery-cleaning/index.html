Renue service

Drapes & upholstery deep cleaning, on-site and in-place.

Experience the convenience and effectiveness of our “Hotel Hygiene System” for drapery cleaning. With Renue Systems, drapery cleaning is done on-site and in-place, eliminating the need for removal from windows. This reduces downtime and ensures consistent room rentability. In just a few minutes, our specialized process leaves drapes clean, fresh-smelling, and ready for occupancy, maintaining the pristine appearance of your rooms.

Upholstery cleaning excellence

At Renue Systems, we take pride in delivering exceptional upholstery cleaning services. Whether it’s sofas in the lobby, guest room furnishings, banquet chairs, or hygienically cleaned mattresses, our professionals pay special attention to detail, ensuring your guests experience the highest levels of comfort and satisfaction.

Specialized equipment and techniques

With over 30 years of expertise in commercial upholstery cleaning, Renue Systems is your trusted partner for superior results. We employ the Hot Water Extraction (HWE) technique, known for its effectiveness in removing soil and maintaining upholstery fibers’ health. Our expert technicians undergo extensive training and possess stain removal expertise, ensuring we can tackle even the toughest stains and eliminate odors effectively.

Extend upholstery lifespan

Deep cleaning upholstery is not just about aesthetics — it’s about prolonging the life of your furnishings and creating a healthier environment for your guests and staff. Our deep cleaning process helps eliminate mold spores, allergens like pollen, and pests like fleas and dust mites, ensuring a safer and more comfortable space for all.

Benefits of Renue Systems upholstery cleaning

  • Extended lifespan of upholstery, saving you money on premature replacements.
  • Enhanced appearance and cleanliness, leaving a lasting impression on guests.
  • Improved air quality and allergen reduction, promoting a healthier environment.
  • Peace of mind for guests, knowing they are sleeping on clean, hygienic mattresses.
