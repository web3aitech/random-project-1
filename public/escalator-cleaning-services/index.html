Renue service

Escalator cleaning that protects a significant investment.

Escalators represent a significant investment for many of our clients, often constituting the most expensive flooring in their facilities. At Renue Systems, we understand the importance of maintaining these assets, which is why we offer specialized escalator cleaning services tailored to your needs.

Benefits of escalator cleaning

Regular cleaning of escalators, inclinators, and moving walkways offers numerous benefits:

  • Removal of oil buildup, reducing the risk of slips and falls
  • Dust removal to prolong equipment life and prevent malfunctions
  • Reduced downtime compared to periodic deep cleaning or repairs
  • No disruption to foot traffic, ensuring smooth operations
  • Enhanced cleanliness appearance, leaving a positive impression on guests

Handrail disinfection

Escalator handrails are high-touch surfaces that require special attention. Our handrail disinfection service utilizes specific cleaning products and techniques to kill bacteria and viruses effectively while preserving the integrity of the materials. Whether performed by our technicians or your maintenance staff, our goal is zero-disruption disinfection for maximum pathogen kill and equipment longevity.

Escalator restoration

High-traffic surfaces like escalators and moving walkways require regular restorative cleaning to maintain their appearance and functionality. Our specialized machines and products are designed to remove dirt, grease, and grime, revitalizing these surfaces to look like new. Whether you’re planning a renovation or establishing a periodic maintenance schedule, our team is ready to provide a free demonstration.

Protect your investment, enhance the appearance of your facility, and prioritize the health of your visitors with Renue Systems’ escalator cleaning services. Contact us today to schedule a consultation or demonstration tailored to your unique operations.
