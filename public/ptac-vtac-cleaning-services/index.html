Renue service

PTAC/VTAC cleaning that improves efficiency and air quality.

PTACs (Packaged Terminal Air Conditioners) and VTACs (Vertical Terminal Air Conditioners) are vital components of the hospitality industry, providing climate control and comfort for guests. However, maintaining these units is essential to ensure their efficiency, lifespan, and air quality within your facility. That’s where Renue Systems steps in with our expert PTAC/VTAC cleaning services.

Comprehensive cleaning solutions

Our PTAC/VTAC cleaning services go beyond surface cleaning. We focus on deep cleaning and preventive maintenance to optimize the performance of your units. From air filter inspections to seasonal deep cleanings of coils, condensers, and blower wheels, we cover every aspect to ensure maximum efficiency and longevity.

Customized cleaning schedules

We understand that every property has unique needs and usage patterns. That’s why we work closely with you to develop a customized cleaning schedule that fits your level of use. Whether you require monthly, seasonal, or periodic cleanings, we ensure minimal downtime and maximum efficiency for your PTAC/VTAC units.

Benefits of the Renue Systems PTAC/VTAC program

  • Improved Efficiency: Our thorough cleaning processes can save up to 20% on electricity costs by enhancing the efficiency of your air conditioning units.
  • Enhanced Performance: With better cooling performance and improved air quality, your guests will enjoy a more comfortable stay without the need for costly coolant changes or unit replacements.
  • Preventive Maintenance: Our knowledgeable technicians provide preventive maintenance that helps avoid major repair and replacement costs, ensuring your PTAC/VTAC units operate smoothly year-round.
  • Expertise and Accountability: With over 30 years of experience serving top hotels, including Marriott, Hilton, and Hyatt, Renue Systems brings unparalleled expertise and accountability to every cleaning project.
  • Flexible Billing and Competitive Pricing: We understand the budget constraints of the hospitality industry and offer flexible billing options to accommodate your needs.
