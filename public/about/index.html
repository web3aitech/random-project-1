FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

About Renue Systems’ Deep Cleaning Services for Hospitality and Commercial Properties

How often are hotel rooms cleaned?

Hotel rooms are typically cleaned daily to ensure a fresh and comfortable environment for guests during their stay. Our deep cleaning services go beyond routine cleaning to provide thorough sanitation and disinfection, ensuring the highest standards of cleanliness for your establishment.

Are there any specific certifications for hotel cleaning services?

Yes. Renue Systems holds certifications and preferred vendor partnerships with leading hospitality organizations including Avendra and CHAMPS Group Purchasing. Our team is trained and certified to meet the strict standards required by major hotel brands.

What distinguishes high-quality cleaning standards in a hotel?

High-quality hotel cleaning goes beyond surface appearance. It includes deep cleaning of carpets, grout, upholstery, HVAC units, kitchens, and fixtures using professional-grade equipment and chemistry. Renue focuses on restorative cleaning that returns assets to like-new condition.

How many team members will be in my property during the cleaning job?

Team size depends on the scope of the project. We work with your property management team to schedule crews that minimize disruption while maximizing efficiency. We can scale from a small specialized crew to a large team for major renovation cleans.

What should I do before the cleaning team arrives?

We coordinate directly with your team to ensure areas are accessible and prepped. For most services, minimal preparation is needed on your end—we handle logistics, equipment setup, and post-clean walkthrough.

What kind of chemicals and machines do you use?

We use proprietary private-blended chemicals specifically formulated for the hospitality industry, combined with our own purpose-built equipment. Our chemistry and machines are safe for guests and staff while delivering superior results compared to off-the-shelf alternatives.
