Renue service

Tile and grout restoration that brings grout back to life.

Are your grout lines a shadow of their former selves, obscured by years of grime? Renue Systems specializes in sophisticated hard-surface maintenance, employing cutting-edge equipment and our exclusive Hotel Hygiene Chemical Blend.

Our manufacturer-approved process delivers almost immediate results, revitalizing your tiled surfaces safely and effectively. Say goodbye to dark grout lines and hello to their original color in record time.

Grout color sealing — a permanent solution

Dirty, uneven grout lines can detract from the appearance of your space and impact inspection scores. Renue Systems offers a lasting solution with our grout color sealing service.

Using a colored resin sealer, we cover grout lines to achieve uniform color and seal out dirt and liquids. Once color sealed, your grout lines require only mild maintenance to keep them looking pristine for years to come.
