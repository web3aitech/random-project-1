Chicago, IL — Deep Cleaning Services

Renue Systems' home office in Lombard, IL serves Chicagoland hotels, resorts, casinos, and commercial venues with 24/7 restorative deep cleaning.

Serving Greater Chicago & Northern Illinois

Our Chicagoland team covers all major hotel corridors, the Magnificent Mile, McCormick Place convention complex, O'Hare and Midway airport-area properties, and the broader suburban market.

As Renue's home office, the Chicago team has direct access to our full technology suite, proprietary chemical lines, and the deepest bench of certified technicians in our network.

  • Tile & grout restoration
  • Marble & stone restoration
  • Commercial kitchen deep cleaning
  • PTAC / VTAC cleaning
  • Drapery & upholstery cleaning
  • Flood clean-up & emergency response
  • Electrostatic disinfection spraying
  • Chandelier & light fixture cleaning
  • Power washing
