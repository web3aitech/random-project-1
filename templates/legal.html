Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · Renue Systems, Inc.

1. Information we collect

When you use our website or submit a contact form, we may collect:

  • Name and contact information (email, phone number, company name)
  • Job title and property type
  • Messages you send us via contact forms
  • Browsing data collected via cookies and analytics tools

2. How we use your information

We use collected information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide requested quotes or demos
  • Send you requested resources (guides, checklists)
  • Communicate about services relevant to your property type
  • Improve our website and marketing based on aggregate analytics

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

3. Cookies and analytics

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to understand how visitors interact with the site. We use Google Analytics to collect aggregated, anonymized data about page views and traffic sources. You may disable cookies in your browser settings; this will not prevent you from using our site.

4. Data retention

We retain contact form submissions and associated data for up to three years, or until you request deletion. Analytics data is retained per the default retention settings of the analytics provider.

5. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete personal information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at contact@renuesystems.com.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information submitted to us, including using HTTPS for all data transmission. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.
