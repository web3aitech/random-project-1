Hotel Carpet Cleaning

Deep restorative cleaning that brings tired carpets back to life — and keeps your guest scores glowing.

Why hotel carpets need more than routine cleaning

High-traffic hotel carpets accumulate embedded dirt, body oils, and microorganisms that surface-level vacuuming can't touch. Over time, this degrades fiber quality, dims appearance, and contributes to odors that guests immediately notice.

Renue's restorative carpet cleaning goes beyond extraction — our proprietary processes and chemicals penetrate deep into the fiber pile, removing what others leave behind and extending carpet service life by years.

What's included

  • Hot water extraction with truck-mount or portable equipment
  • Pre-treatment of high-traffic and stained areas
  • Proprietary fiber-safe detergents and deodorizers
  • Spot treatment for set-in stains
  • Speed-dry process to minimize room downtime
  • Post-cleaning fiber protection application (optional)
