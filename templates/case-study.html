Delivering Pristine Results for The Ritz-Carlton

How Renue helped a premier Ritz-Carlton property achieve results that earned praise from the brand's corporate inspection team.

The Ritz-Carlton

Luxury Hotel Group — Carpet & Tile Restoration

Luxury Hotels Carpet Cleaning Tile & Grout

The Challenge

A prominent Ritz-Carlton property was preparing for a high-stakes corporate inspection. The property's carpets and tile surfaces had degraded significantly from heavy foot traffic and years of routine-only cleaning. Previous vendors had been unable to restore them to brand standard.

The Director of Housekeeping needed a trusted partner who could deliver measurable, visible results quickly — and without disrupting guest operations during regular business hours.

The Solution

Renue deployed a team of certified technicians to assess the property and develop a phased restoration plan. Using our proprietary hot-water extraction system and fiber-safe chemical line, we tackled the highest-traffic areas first, scheduling work in off-peak hours to minimize disruption.

For tile and grout surfaces, our technicians applied a multi-step restoration process — removing embedded soil, restoring color consistency, and sealing surfaces for long-term protection.

The Results

The property passed its Ritz-Carlton corporate inspection with high marks in cleanliness. Feedback from the inspection team specifically called out the improved condition of carpets and flooring as notable improvements.

"I cannot thank you enough for all your assistance to make the hotel look pristine! We received many great comments about how clean the hotel felt by the Ritz-Carlton Corporate Team! You are a great partner that I value every day! Thank you and I look forward to continuing a great partnership!"

— Ritz-Carlton Corporate Team

Ongoing partnership

Following the initial project, the Ritz-Carlton property established an ongoing maintenance contract with Renue, scheduling quarterly deep-cleaning visits to maintain brand standards year-round.
